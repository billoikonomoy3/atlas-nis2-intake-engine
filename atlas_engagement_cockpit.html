1 · Evidence intake Stage 4

Tick the evidence actually received, split into design (the control as written) and operating (proof it ran over the period). 3 · EQCR challenge Stage 6

A four-eyes Engagement Quality Control Reviewer tries to break every PASS — run on every control, every time, for free. Caps an over-claimed level, escalates an unprovenanced or untested conclusion, flags a governance gap as a board-liability vector. A firm runs this a few times because senior time is scarce; Atlas runs it exhaustively.
